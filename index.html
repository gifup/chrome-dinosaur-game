About the Game

The Chrome Dinosaur Game became one of Google's most popular hidden features. Originally created as an offline browser game, it has become a favorite endless runner enjoyed by millions of players around the world.

You can also play the Chrome Dinosaur Game online without disabling your internet connection.

How to Play

Jump

Press Space or the Up Arrow to jump over cacti.

Duck

Use the Down Arrow to duck under flying birds.

Survive

Stay alive as long as possible and beat your best score.
